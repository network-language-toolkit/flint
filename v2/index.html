Hundreds of thousands of emails retrieved through FOIA, finally searchable

Completely free and open to all

ℹ️ About the page images shown with each result

Each search result is shown with one or more page images from the original FOIA release. The mapping from email to page is approximate for some results.

  • ~82% of results have a verified page association (their body text was found in the source OCR).
  • ~18% are approximate — flagged inline with ⚠️ — because the email's body could not be uniquely matched (empty body, one-line reply, or OCR/clean-text divergence). For these, the page/thread is inferred from neighboring emails.

Thread boundaries themselves come from authoritative upstream OCR metadata (page_number on each page); only the email → thread assignment is the approximate step.
